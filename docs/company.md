    Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, build, and deploy AI solutions for various applications.
    Assist in data preparation, model training, and algorithm selection under the guidance of engineer mentors.
    Conduct experiments and tests to evaluate the performance of AI models and optimize them for production.
    Write clean and maintainable code that supports the development and deployment of AI systems.
    Stay updated with the latest AI trends and technologies to bring innovative ideas to the team.


Selection Process

The Qualcomm AI Residency Program employs a rigorous multi-stage selection process. The standard procedure includes the following rounds:

    Application

    Portfolio review

    Technical interview

Applicants eligible for the fast-track process will be contacted separately.

    A letter of recommendation is optional but strongly encouraged.
